The custom lettering was created in collaboration between Wire Design and illustrator Mads Burchart. It spells out Wire name and should always be used to represent the brand.

Certain things should never be done with Wire logo.

  • No bending or twisting
  • No distorting or stretching
  • No rotating or tilting
  • No using on a photo
  • No using in multiple colors
  • No special effects

There are no specific size limitations to displaying the logo as it is designed to be legible even at very small sizes. You should leave it room to breathe, though. To do that, avoid placing objects in an area around it.

It is OK to use the logo in any color but the simplest is to go for just black or white. Using it with any of Wire official colors also works well.

Our logo is custom lettering created in collaboration between Wire Design and illustrator Mads Burchart. It spells out our name and should always be used to represent the Wire brand.

PDF ↓ SVG ↓ PNG ↓ JPG ↓

We are happy to see Wire logo everywhere, but there are certain things you should never do with it.

  • No bending or twisting
  • No distorting or stretching
  • No rotating or tilting
  • No using on a photo
  • No using in multiple colors
  • No special effects

There are also no size limitations to displaying the logo as it is designed to be legible even at very small sizes. We do ask you to give it some room to breathe, though, so avoid placing objects in an area around it.

Wire logo is also not limited to be used in specific color, but we often prefer simple black or white. Using it with any Wire official colors will work well.

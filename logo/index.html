The custom lettering was created in collaboration between Wire Design and illustrator Mads Burcharth. It spells out Wire name and should always be used to represent the brand.

Things to never do with Wire logo.

  • Don’t bend or twist
  • Don’t distort or stretch
  • Don’t rotate or tilt
  • Don’t use on a photo
  • Don’t use in multiple colors
  • Don’t apply effects

There are no size limits to displaying the logo as it is designed to be legible even when very small. You should leave it room to breathe by not placing objects in an area around it.

It is fine to use the logo in any color but the simplest is to go for just black or white. Using it with any of Wire palette colors also works well.
